Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that is made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich, creamy mascarpone mixture. It is a popular dessert that is loved by many for its light and airy texture and delicious coffee flavor. Tiramisu is a perfect dessert for any occasion, whether it's a dinner party, a holiday celebration, or just a sweet treat for yourself.

There are many variations of tiramisu, but the most common type is made with ladyfingers, espresso coffee, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, and cocoa powder. The ladyfingers are soaked in coffee and layered with a mixture of mascarpone cheese, eggs, and sugar. The dessert is then chilled and dusted with cocoa powder before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. In another bowl, beat together mascarpone, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until combined.
  4. In a small bowl, combine coffee and coffee liqueur.
