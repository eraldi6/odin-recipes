Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that consists of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is a versatile and customizable dish that can be enjoyed in many different ways, from classic Margherita to gourmet combinations.

Originating from Naples, Italy, pizza has become a global phenomenon with countless regional variations. Whether you prefer a thin and crispy crust or a thick and chewy one, there's a pizza style for everyone. It's a perfect meal for any occasion, whether it's a casual dinner, a party, or a quick snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). If you're using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the Parmesan cheese.
  4. Arrange the pepperoni, olives, bell peppers, and red onions over the cheese. Sprinkle the oregano and basil on top.
  5. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza. If you're using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the hot stone in the oven. If you're using a baking sheet, place the sheet in the oven.
  6. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
